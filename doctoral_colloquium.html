IEEE MDM 2024: Call for Doctoral Colloquium Submissions

Following the success of previous years, MDM 2024 will host a Doctoral Colloquium on Mobile Data Management. The Colloquium aims at providing PhD students and recent PhD graduates with an opportunity to present their dissertation research, recently accomplished or still ongoing, in all areas of mobile data management. MDM 2024 offers an excellent opportunity for PhD students to network directly with researchers, exchange ideas, receive constructive feedback and guidance, as well as learn about emerging problems tackled by the community.

Doctoral Colloquium

The Doctoral Colloquium will be organised as a regular session at MDM 2024 and attended by a broad audience, including experts in mobile data management from industry, academia, and government. During the Colloquium, each presenter will have a brief presentation. At the same time, the community's senior members will provide constructive feedback. Best presentation awards will be given to the most innovative ideas/efforts presented at the Colloquium.

Submission Guidelines

We encourage submissions from students currently enrolled in doctoral programs and recent graduates who completed their PhD dissertations after July 2022. Submissions should be single-authored, while contributions of advisors and co-authors can be acknowledged in the abstract.

The page limit is three pages, including references. A peer-review process will ensure the quality and relevance of the proposed manuscript. The accepted submissions will appear in the proceedings of MDM 2024.

All submissions need to follow IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines. Please use the following URL for paper submissions: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/mdm2024/

The Review Process will be single-blind (i.e., the reviewers are not identified).
