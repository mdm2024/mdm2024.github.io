IEEE MDM 2024: Call for Industry and Application Track Papers

Continuing its history, MDM 2024 Industry, Systems and Application Track solicits submissions of papers describing original contributions in developing and applying mobile computing and data management technologies towards the myriad of real- world problems. We encourage industrial and academic researchers and practitioners to share and discuss the challenges and solutions in complex real-world systems, as well as the practices and examples in commercial use cases. Topics of interest include, but not limited to:

  • Theoretical Foundations of Data-intensive Mobile Computing
  • Data Sensing Techniques in Mobile Computing
  • Mobile Crowd-Sourcing and Crowd-Sensing
  • Mobile Data Governance and Cleaning
  • Mobile Data Management and Applications
  • Data Security and Privacy in Mobile Data Computing
  • Machine Learning/AI for Mobile Data
  • Mobile Data Visualization Systems and Applications
  • Novel Mobile Data Processing Tools/Middleware
  • Location-Based Services and Applications
  • Urban Computing Applications
  • Contact tracing and object tracking
  • Spatio-temporal sensor data processing

Submission Guidelines

  • Page limit: 8 pages, including all figures, tables, and references.
  • Please use the following URL for paper submissions: https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/mdm2024/
  • All submissions need to follow IEEE Computer Society Proceedings Manuscript Formatting Guidelines. See templates here.

Note that a paper exceeding the page limit in the respective category may be rejected without review. If there are any appendices, they are counted within the page limit.

Submission of a meaningful abstract by the abstract deadline is a precondition for paper submission.

The Review Process will be single-blind (i.e., the reviewers are not identified).
