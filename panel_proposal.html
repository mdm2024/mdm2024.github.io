IEEE MDM 2024: Call for Panel Proposals

We solicit proposals for panels at the 25th IEEE International Conference on Mobile Data Management, July 3-6, 2023 in Brussels.

Panel proposals are expected to address new, exciting, and controversial issues of broad interest to the community. The proposed panel should be provocative, informative, and entertaining.

Panel proposals should include the following:

  • Panel Title
  • Panel Abstract (not more than half a page)
  • Prospective Questions
  • Organizers

The intended length of the panel is 1 hour and the anticipated format is a panel with on-site participation.

An inclusive and diverse mix (e.g., gender, age, region) of panelists is encouraged. Representation from both industry and academia and panelists from different research communities are also encouraged.

The panel abstract or extended panel description will be included in the proceedings (not more than 4 pages).

Please email your proposals to Demetris Zeinalipour (dzeina@ucy.ac.cy) and Daniela Nicklas (daniela.nicklas@uni-bamberg.de). Each submitted proposal will be verified by email.

Important dates

Proposal submission: March 28, 2023
Acceptance notification (tentative): March 31, 2023
Camera-ready due (tentative): April 28, 2023
